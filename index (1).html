How it works

3 steps. Built for speed and clarity.

  1. Request — location + car details + what’s wrong.
  2. Match — we show a mechanic card with direct call options.
  3. Confirm — you get next steps, and an admin inbox records the request.

Why this is legit

  • Transparent contact: you see numbers and hours before calling.
  • Privacy-first: your phone is used only to connect the job.
  • Minimal: small UI, fast load, built for later scale.
